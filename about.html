About Us

Tropique is a brand dedicated to merging traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design, specializing in high-quality, eco-friendly bags. Our products not only reflect a commitment to sustainability but also celebrate the skills of local artisans. By offering stylish accessories that are both elegant and environmentally conscious. Tropique aims to redefine fashion through sustainable practices.

Our Mission and Vision

Tropique is committed to creating stylish, sustainable products that honor and support local artisans. We strive to provide eco-conscious consumers with high-quality accessories that reflect elegance, authenticity, and innovation.

To be a leading brand in sustainable fashion, recognized for our dedication to craftsmanship, environmental responsibility, and the celebration of cultural heritage. Tropique envisions a world where fashion and sustainability coexist harmoniously, inspiring consumers to make mindful choices that benefit both people and the planet.
